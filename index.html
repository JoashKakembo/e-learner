Who we Are

e-Learner Uganda, established in 2012, initially focused on providing Computers 4 Kids program to schools. Later shifted to offering ICT training services to teachers and students, collaborating with KCCA and UICT. Expanded to strategic planning for ICT integration in Solomon Islands. Partnered with U-Connect to provide affordable laptops pre-loaded with RACHEL-Plus, an offline digitized education content system.
